CustomerManagerDelegate

@objc public protocol CustomerManagerDelegate

CustomerManagerDelegate protocol defines the methods that a delegate of a CustomerManager object must adopt. All methods of the protocol are required.

  • customer(_:readyToCommunicate:)

    Invoked when the customer’s device connected to merchant’s device and they are ready to exchange data.

    Note

    All methods of CustomerManager, except setIdentity(_:), enableDiscoverable() and disableDiscoverable(), must be called after this method is invoked.

    Declaration

    Parameters

    ready

    The readiness to exchange data.

    Invoked when the customer is in charge of making transaction.

    Note

    After making transaction, should call updateTransactionStatus(_:) of CustomerManager to notify merchant status of the transaction.
